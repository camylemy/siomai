VISION AND MISSION

VISION

                               

To redefine contemporary fashion by merging individuality, creativity, and quality craftsmanship, QU4DRO envisions becoming a global symbol of innovation and authenticity. Rooted in the power of collaboration, we strive to inspire confidence and self-expression in every piece, reflecting the dynamic synergy of our team and the diverse stories of those who wear our designs.

MISSION

                               

The purpose of this clothing brand is to give clothes to our fellow Filipinos. This is all for them so that they can afford stylish clothes at a cheaper and affordable price, because not all people can buy whatever they want especially clothes. Clothes have a lot of good brands but its way too expensive and so we created this brand to make fashion-like clothes at a cheaper price. The whole purpose of this is to make our country look good.

HISTORY

The games were our inspiration and because of the game we had an agreement to make a jersey. (KWADRO) There are many group games and groups need a trademark by which when they are seen, they will be immediately recognized. This jersey can be used in br games where 4 people, That's why the name became kwadro meaning four players. not just 4 players but being strong and trusting each other. We "QU4DRO" clothing brand started as a small thrift shop before we realized, we can just make our own clothing brand and so we 4 students (Renzo, Eldrin, Daniel and Jerick), started our journey as a small clothing shop owner. The purpose of our shop was to give fashion to our fellow Filipinos at a cheap price so that we can afford elegant style clothes and the citizens of our country will look good
